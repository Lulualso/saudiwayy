About This Place

Al-Diriyah is a town on the northwestern outskirts of Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, and the original home of the Saudi royal family. It was the capital of the first Saudi state and is now one of the most important heritage sites in the kingdom. The At-Turaif district, a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 2010, features remarkable Najdi architectural style buildings dating back to the 18th century.
